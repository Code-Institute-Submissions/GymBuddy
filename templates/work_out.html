Workout Structure

How to structure the workout

Complete the coloured excercise as a pair for 3 rounds. For example if Clams and mountain climbers are the both blue then those two excercises are a round. Repeat that pair of excercise 3 times. This is only relevant for warm-ups and sprints


The main excercises are to be done in reps as outlined by the numbers beside them. You can record the weight of your main lifts by selecting the "Weight" button.


The cooldown is an essentail part of training and will aid mobility. 20 seconds per cool down is recommended.
